# COSMOLOGY — GOLDEN GATES AND THE 10 HEAVENS
## CoinorChronicles Style Guide · Version 3.0 · May 2026
### The infinite journey structure that makes every episode matter.

---

## THE CORE IDEA

Every character in the Realm of Coinor is on an infinite journey toward **Golden Gates** — price milestones that represent transcendence to a new level of power.

Between each Gate lie **10 Heavens** — sub-milestones. Every Heaven is tracked. Every Heaven ascension is acknowledged in the story. Every Gate crossing is a legendary event with a full celebration episode.

**Why this matters:**
- The audience always knows exactly where their favorite character stands
- Every price movement is meaningful — it's progress or retreat on the road
- The journey never ends — there is always a next Heaven, always a next Gate
- Milestones create **moments** — shareable, memorable, community-building events

## HOW TO CALCULATE HEAVEN POSITION

Heaven 1 begins at the Gate price.
Heaven 10 ends just below the next Gate price (approximately 90% of the way to the next Gate).
Each Heaven spans 1/10th of the distance between the two Gates.

**Formula:**
```
Heaven_N_floor = Gate_1_price + ((Gate_2_price - Gate_1_price) / 10) × (N - 1)
Heaven_N_ceiling = Gate_1_price + ((Gate_2_price - Gate_1_price) / 10) × N
```

**Example (BTC, Gate 1 = $100k, Gate 2 = $1M):**
- Gap = $900,000
- Each Heaven = $90,000 wide
- Heaven 1: $100k – $190k
- Heaven 2: $190k – $280k
- Heaven 5: $460k – $550k
- Heaven 10: $910k – $1M

## THE REALM CARD SYSTEM

### Heaven Cards:
- Issued at every Heaven ascension
- Design: Glowing ancient coin, soft light, character symbol
- Colour: Silver-bronze glow
- Text: "Heaven [N] — [Character Name] — [Date] — [Price]"
- Used as: Social media celebration posts, future physical merch, future NFT

### Gate Cards:
- Issued at every Gate crossing — legendary events
- Design: Full gold glow, ornate, dramatic
- Colour: Pure gold with character colours
- Text: "Gate [N] Reached — [Character Name] — [Date] — [Price] — The Fellowship Witnesses"
- Used as: Full celebration episode centrepiece, premium merchandise, high-value NFT

### Celebration Episode Trigger:
When the system detects a Gate or Heaven crossing in Airtable/MySQL:
1. Standard story is paused for that clan
2. Celebration episode generated using `celebration_episode.md` prompt
3. Special visual treatment applied
4. Realm Card displayed at dramatic peak of video
5. Realm Card posted across all channels as a standalone image

## NARRATIVE USE OF COSMOLOGY

### Referencing Heavens in Standard Episodes:
- "Legolas stands firm in Heaven 3 of his first Gate — the road to $1,000 grows shorter."
- "Merry has not moved from Heaven 2. The ambush holds him. But he does not retreat."
- "Aragorn, still reclaiming Heaven 1 after the wars, moves 4 gold units closer tonight."

### When a Character is Far from Their Gate:
- Do not say "they have a long way to go" in a discouraging tone
- Say: "The road ahead is long. Aragorn has walked longer roads."

### When Multiple Characters Move Simultaneously:
- Open with the most significant movement
- Give each character one line minimum
- Close with Aragorn's synthesis observation
