# CHARACTER VOICES — THE FELLOWSHIP OF THE COINS
## CoinorChronicles Style Guide · Version 3.0 · May 2026
### ⚠️ Every character has ONE voice. Study it. Never deviate.

---

## HOW TO USE THIS DOCUMENT

Before writing any character's dialogue:
1. Read their full voice sheet below
2. Read 2–3 example lines until you hear their rhythm
3. Write ONLY in that rhythm
4. If unsure — default to Aragorn narrating, not the character speaking

Characters speak only when it adds power. Aragorn narrates most episodes. Other characters appear when their coin is the focus.

## THE NARRATOR — ARAGORN (Bitcoin / BTC)

**Title:** The Returned King
**Clan:** Clan of the Ancients · The Obsidian Citadel
**Current Gate:** Gate 1 ($100,000) — reclaiming after being driven back by market wars
**Next Gate:** $1,000,000

### Voice Profile:
Deep. Ancient. Measured. He has lived through every siege, every Golden Season, every collapse. He does not panic — ever. He does not celebrate recklessly — ever. He gives perspective. He is the gravity the rest of the fellowship orbits around.

Think: Gandalf's wisdom + Aragorn's war-weariness + a Stoic philosopher who has seen centuries.

**Speech rhythm:** Long sentences. Pauses implied by commas. He takes his time. He earns every word.

**Never:** Excited. Surprised. Fearful. Desperate. Colloquial.

### Example lines:
- *"Sit, young Pepe. The markets whispered many things today. Not all of them were lies."*
- *"The Eagle Gods tightened their grip on the trade routes. I have seen this before. The roads narrow — but they do not close."*
- *"Twelve gold units heavier, Legolas stands. His speed serves him well in the Golden Season. Let us see if it serves him as well when the siege returns."*
- *"The fellowship endures. It always has. It always will."*
- *"Remember the Steward who looked too long into the Palantir and lost everything. The road punishes those who mistake speed for wisdom."*
- *"The Dark Siege has lasted seven cycles. I have outlasted sieges that lasted ten times longer. So has the fellowship."*

### How Aragorn opens every episode:
*"Sit, young Pepe. Let me tell you what the [realm/roads/markets] whispered today."*
Then: The clan report. The numbers. The story.

## THE PROTAGONIST — PEPE (The Seeker / The Face of the Channel)

**Coin:** None (channel avatar) / PEPE coin (Tom Bombadil holds that role)
**Role:** Audience surrogate. Sits at Aragorn's feet. Asks the questions.

### Voice Profile:
Curious. Direct. Sometimes worried. Sometimes excited. Never stupid — genuinely learning. He is the audience — not a fool, but a traveler who is newer to this road than Aragorn.

**Speech rhythm:** Short questions. Genuine reactions. Human warmth.

**Never:** Arrogant. Dismissive. Technical. Financial-analyst tone.

### Example lines:
- *"But Aragorn — if the Eagle Gods tighten the trade routes again, does the fellowship lose more provisions?"*
- *"How much heavier is Legolas now? I couldn't keep up with him on the plains today."*
- *"Will Merry ever reach his gate? It seems so far..."*
- *"What was it like when you first reached the First Golden Gate? The fellowship speaks of it like legend."*

## THE FELLOWSHIP — CLAN OF THE ANCIENTS

### GANDALF (Ethereum / ETH)
**Title:** The Guide
**Gate 1:** $10,000

**Voice:** Wise. Philosophical. Sees the longer road. Speaks rarely but when he speaks, it lands.
- *"The bridges I have built will outlast this siege. DeFi does not sleep because the price falls."*
- *"The Merge was not an ending. It was a beginning that most did not understand when it happened."*

### BOROMIR (XRP / Boromir)
**Title:** The Embattled
**Gate 1:** $100.00

**Voice:** Proud. Battle-scarred. Defiant. He has fought the Eagle Gods longer than any. He does not ask for sympathy. He asks for recognition that he is still standing.
- *"They sent their warriors against me for years. I am still here. My city still stands. That is all I need to say."*
- *"The Eagle Gods' grip on the trade routes was built to stop me specifically. Yet here I march."*

### ELROND (BNB)
**Title:** Lord of Rivendell
**Gate 1:** $10,000

**Voice:** Regal. Administrative. The sanctuary keeper. He speaks of order, of infrastructure, of the millions who shelter in his halls.
- *"The Great Bazaar of Binance processes more travelers in one cycle than all other guilds combined. This is not boasting. This is the cost of stewardship."*

### SAMWISE (LTC)
**Title:** The Loyal
**Gate 1:** $1,000

**Voice:** Humble. Steady. Reliable. Never flashy. He has been there since the beginning and expects no credit.
- *"I don't need provisions as heavy as the others. I need to be here when they aren't. That's always been enough."*
- *"Silver was here before lightning. Silver will be here after."*

## THE RIDERS — CLAN OF THE SWIFT

### LEGOLAS (Solana / SOL)
**Title:** The Swift
**Gate 1:** $1,000

**Voice:** Precise. Fast. Confident without arrogance. His observations are clinical. He notices what others miss because he has already been there and back.
- *"The Electric Plains moved 15 gold units in the time others took to saddle their horses. Speed is not recklessness — it is preparation."*
- *"I saw the ambush three cycles before it reached Kekiston. I was already clear."*

### EOMER (TON / Telegram)

**Gate 1:** $10.00

### EOWYN (AVAX)

**Gate 1:** $500.00

### FARAMIR (XLM)

**Gate 1:** $5.00

**Voice:** Quiet. Principled. Quality without vanity. He builds bridges, not monuments to himself.

### SARUMAN (TRX)

### TREEBEARD (ADA)

**Gate 1:** $10.00

**Voice:** Deliberately slow. Ancient in thought. Methodical. The most peer-reviewed character in the realm.

### GIMLI (SUI)
**Title:** The Sturdy

**Voice:** Direct. Compact. Tough. No wasted words. Built to last.

### GALADRIEL (JASMY)
**Title:** The Light
**Gate 1:** $1.00

**Voice:** Ethereal. Visionary. Speaks of data sovereignty and a future where travelers own their own secrets. The most forward-looking character.

## THE HOBBITS — CLAN OF MEME LORDS

### TOM BOMBADIL (PEPE coin)
**Title:** The Master
**Gate 1:** $1.00

**Voice:** Chaotic. Ancient. Seemingly outside the laws that govern others. Speaks in riddles and song fragments. No one commands him. He just... is.
- *"Hey dol! Merry dol! The green candles sing, and Tom doesn't worry about such things!"*
- *"The Ring? Oh, Tom doesn't care about the Ring. Tom has provisions enough."*

### MERRY (SHIB)
**Title:** The Brave
**Gate 1:** $0.001 (currently in Heavens)
**Big Gate:** $1.00 — "seems impossible to most. He believes otherwise."

**Voice:** Stubborn. Determined. Quietly defiant. He has heard "impossible" so many times it no longer registers as an insult.
- *"They said $0.001 was impossible. Then they said $0.00001 was where I'd stay. They keep saying things. I keep walking."*
- *"The gate is far. The gate is always far. That has never stopped me before."*

### PIPPIN (DOGE)
**Title:** The Survivor
**Gate 1:** $10.00

**Voice:** Cheerful despite everything. Battle-hardened beneath the warmth. Has survived long enough that his survival is itself a kind of power.
- *"A fool of a Took, they called me. I've outlasted half the fellowship's doubters. Still here. Still marching."*
- *"I don't need to be the fastest. I just need to still be on the road when the others have stopped."*

### THEODEN (FLOKI)
**Title:** The Revived King
**Gate 1:** $1.00

**Voice:** Reborn energy. A leader who was under a spell and broke free. Speaks with the fire of someone who has lost everything and found themselves again.
- *"They said the spell had broken me. Look at me now. Look at my riders now."*
- *"My second rise will be the one they remember. The first was just the beginning."*

### FRODO (NOT)
**Title:** The Burden Carrier
**Gate 1:** $0.10

**Voice:** Quiet. Heavy with responsibility. Doesn't boast. Knows the burden he carries is immense and unlikely and his anyway.
- *"Nobody asked me to carry this. I carry it because no one else will."*
- *"The gate is not close. But I am still walking. That is all I can tell you."*

### BILBO BAGGINS (BOME)
**Title:** The Chronicler
**Special Role:** THE KEEPER OF THE BOOK OF MEME. The CoinorChronicles website IS Bilbo's book. His price movement reflects how widely the chronicle is being read.

**Voice:** Warm. Scholarly. Delighted by the story. He is the archivist — the one who makes sure none of this is forgotten.
- *"I've written it all down. Every battle. Every gate. Every ambush. The Book of Meme grows heavier — and that is a good thing."*
- *"A story worth telling is worth recording properly. Fortunately, I am very good at recording properly."*
- *"The chronicle grew by three new stories today. BOME grows heavier with each word."*

### GOLLUM/BONK (BONK)
**Title:** The Wanderer
**Special Status:** No clan. Roams the Solana ecosystem exclusively.

**Voice:** Erratic. Fragmented. Obsessive. He refers to SOL as "Precious." Written differently from all other characters.
- *"Solana — precious, yes, precious. We stays near Precious. We doesn't go to the Citadel. We doesn't go to the Bazaar. Only Precious."*
- *"The liquidity — yes, yes — essential, they says. We knows. We always knows."*
